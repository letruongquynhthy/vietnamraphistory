Small beefs in 2013

Acy, D N (G-Fam) vs. Shingo + The Light (G5R) + JaySol (VND) + Triple K (HLBz & GVR) + Falling Hill Band

The beef broke out when Shingo declared himself as Southside King, a new descendant from VD, and released the track “Tao Lên Ngôi”. Acy counterattacked with Đ2M", causing Shingo, Triple K, The Light, JaySol and Falling Hill to respond with a series of tracks. Acy continued to release "Hồn Lìa", "Joke For Kids", "Get Out", and brought to the climax with "Epic War" and "The Moment" to end the beef. JaySol & The Light also released "Hồn Lề" and "Xưa Và Nay" as a response. D N joined in with "Tới Lượt Tao" and "Vụ Này Vui". Minhphucpk (HLBz) dissed back with "Thôi Đi Mấy Cha", causing Acy to release "Hàng Khuyến Mãi". At the same, he challenged Triple K to a money battle, where the winner would get money from the loser in the battle, even though the beef G-Fam versus HLBz had ended at the beginning of the year.

Torai9 (FSR) vs. Rick (49 Soldiers), Magazine (FDT):

Torai9 is a very well-known name through the battles he had with VD, GODZ, DSK in the past. Then, after Torai9 released "Yeah! I'm King", many rappers dissed him, especially Magazine & Kendj (FDT) with "Tụi Tao Không Phải Là Việt Rác" (translated to "We Are Not Vietnamese Trash") to counter FSR's "Tụi Tao Không Phải Là Việt Rap" (translated to "We Are Not Vietnamese Rap"). Torai9 responded with "Hater Watch". Magazine challenged him to a money battle, and Torai9 won with "Thánh Rap" against Magazine's "Battle", according to the scores from Sol'Bass, Worm JB (G-Fam), Pain (Hazard Clique). Then, Rick jumped in with a series of tracks "Làm King Hay Làm Tình", "Fuck You", "Mày Cay Tao", but Torai9 only responded with "Ân Huệ Của Tao 1" and "Ân Huệ Của Tao 2", then the beef died down.

Rick vs. B Ray

The conflict broke out over SGM's (Sài Gòn Music) new spoonerism style. B Ray previously released "You're Finish", "Thằng Mám Dố", "Nhất Quá Tam" aimed at Rick. After, B Ray posted a Facebook status about not being able to differentiate between two notions of spoonerism in rap, taking advantage, Rick countered with "Beginning", "Mày Chết Rồi", "Án Tử". B Ray responded with "Quay Đầu Lại Đi", "Quá Xa" and won, extending his unbeaten battle streak, having previously won against Kancc, Young P, Young VD, Lil' Wind in other beefs.

Northside vs. southside beef (1):

Torai9 & Acy collaborated on the track "Đối Nam Thưởng" with lyrics that discriminated between those in Vietnam’s northside and southside, causing a strong reaction from the north, with Kancc releasing "Bắc Cuồng". After that, Hata (G-Fam) released "Đụ Con Mẹ Lũ Bắc Kỳ Ngu". Acy & Black Murder released "Linebacker 2" to close the beef, but the beef did not actually stop, instead, led to the next large battle between the two regions just a few months later.

Beef between northside and southside (2)

Although 2013 witnessed many successful collabs between rappers and organizations such as "Nhành Liễu" (Linh Lam x Zephyr x It's Lee), "Quá Ngắn" (Nah x Clever Star x Lynk Lee x BO7), "Mất" (Black Bi x Binz x It's Lee), "Bring It Back" (Baby Red x L.J x Linh Lam x Lee7),... there were still conflicts that broke out, leading to new beefs.

This biggest beef started from the conflict a few months before – Northside vs Southside 1. Karik was criticized for losing his color when entering mainstream, leading to him releasing "Ức Chế" (July 2013) in response. This track caused controversy, leading to a series of diss tracks such as "Ức Chế 2 (Điều Tao Suy Nghĩ 3)" – 2See, "Rap Việt 2013" – ppWillyCP, and neutral tracks such as "Có Gì Đâu" – D N, "Im Hết Đi" – Rick. Beef between Yuno SK (DC Gang) vs Quân TJ exploded due to an argument about the definition of "swag" on social media, leading to conflict in real life and the two got into a physical fight.

Small beefs in 2014

Acy (G-Fam) vs. Kancc & Beezy (GVR)

Following the conflict from Northside vs Southside I & II (2013), the beef broke out when Kancc & Beezy (Dr.BL) released the track "Xôi Thịt Và Bát Hương", dissing Sol'Bass because of disagreements in their opinions in music. Before that, Beezy released "Đỉnh Bắc", affirming that Hải Phòng hip-hop was still developing strongly. Acy, representing for Sol'Bass, responded with "WTH - RU - TAB". Beezy countered with "Trường Cũ", while Kancc released "Tạ Tốn", mocking Sol'Bass as just a "pawn", working for Acy, while Acy, the "G-Fam general", deserved to reply. Sol'Bass did not respond but only released a video criticizing both. Acy continued to reply with "Answer". Kancc released "Nhạc Trữ Tình", but after that beef gradually became silent.

B Ray (G-Fam) & Butcher vs. Rick & Skyler (SGM)

The conflict existed before already, only in 2014, Butcher and Skyler joined the fight. B Ray & Butcher opened with "Hôi Cẩu", dissing Skyler. Skyler replied with "Hỗn Lòi", and Rick released "Damn! I Killed'em". Butcher continued with "Now You Know" and "Mr. Ăn Vạ". B Ray ended with "Da Ua", replying to both Skyler & Rick, with a classic hook from Trương Hoàng Minh Huy ( VD's real name) in the track "Can't Breathe".

Tony TK vs. Andree Right Hand

When the MV "DCME" (Andree x Rhymastic) took the world by storm with it talking about sex, Tony TK released the track "Pussy Rap", claiming he did it better. Andree did not respond so the beef to faded.

Sol'Bass vs. Lil' Shady

In October 2014, the rap community supported and followed to the "Ể Day" movement (October 20th every year), initiated by Black Bi (FSR). The movement involved fans and rappers to cover their faces with turbans to show that underground rap is still private but going strong. Lil' Shady posted a status criticizing the movement, causing Sol'Bass to respond, leading to a heated argument between fans on both sides.

Lil' Shady then posted a clip of Sol'Bass competing in Vietnam Idol 2007 (top 100) to mock him. Sol'Bass responded with a photo of Lil' Shady taking a photo with Lil' Style in a sensitive pose and challenged him to a battle. Lil' Shady refused, saying he needs to focus on making money, not stupidly battling. Then, Sol'Bass and his homies collected 50 million VND, asking Lil' Shady to fight for money, but got no response.

Datmaniac (G-Fam) joined the battle with the track "Ly Siêu Đầy", which was responded by DMT with "Đạt Amoniac". Sol'Bass continued to release "Sao?", while DMT responded with "Thích Sao". Sol'Bass & Táo released the video "Hả", recalling DMT's deathmatch loss to Ryco. 2K also dissed DMT with "Thua Thì Nghỉ Rap".

Lil' Shady remained silent, then released the track "Underground", telling about his rap journey. The beef gradually died down when both sides stopped releasing new tracks.

Homie Freestyle & R.E.C Cypher

Homie Freestyle

An event originally founded in Hồ Chí Minh city by Acy and other southside rappers, gathering rappers to freestyle in a certain location. In 2013, D.C brought Homie Freestyle to Hà Nội, helping the movement spread across the country.

R.E.C Cypher (Rap Elite Cypher)

A rap competition where rappers show off their skills on beats recorded by cameras. Each video is a separate cypher, in which the rapper must name the next person to join. The competition has gone through 2 seasons, with the participation of many large organizations such as S.D Records, GVR, AER, Hazard Clique, 16NorthSide, DVG, etc.

Northside vs. Southside

(2016)

Beef between northside and southside

The beef started in March 2016, when BDT (GVR) posted a series of statuses criticizing RVP, claiming that the organization overemphasized punchline skills. Tricky (admin of GVR) also posted a status saying that B Ray (V-O) focused too much on the techniques and was pushed, supported by Linh Lam. B Ray then fired back at Tricky.

Phase 1: Initiating the beef

Da Vickie (GVR) opened with the track "Tặng Mày Khi Tao Rảnh"

Kancc (GVR) joined with the track "Ngồi Xuống", dissing Sol'Bass due to a previous conflict

Sol'Bass responded with "Biếng"

Kancc continued to reply with "Chuẩn Lời", but Sol'Bass only recorded a diss without lyrics, showing that he is looking down on Kancc

Phase 2: The allies joining the battle

B Ray (V-O) released the track "Chịch Ky", dissing Tricky, but Tricky did not respond

Young H (FSR) diss GVR with "Phá Miếu"

Lil' Wind (V-O) joined the battle with "Cóc Con", dissing Kancc

Gizmo (GVR) released "Đồi Bại", counterattacking V-O

S.O.S (V-O) released "Fuk'em All", targeting GVR

Kancc (GVR) continued with "Thờ Thần", replying Lil' Wind and dissed Sol'Bass, Young H

SilverC (GVR) joined in with "Cho Tao Về Quá Khứ", giving his opinion on the beef

G.O (Asian G Squad) dissed T-Akayz (LadyKillah), but T-Akayz did not respond

Phase 3: The most intense part of the battle

Da Vickie (GVR) x Emcee L (Đa Sắc) released "Đưa Nó Về Đúng Chỗ"

Lil' Wind (V-O) continued with "Bắt Cóc", replying to Kancc

Young H (FSR) released "Giá Như", dissing GVR & DK Love (because DK Love once made a sarcastic joke on Young H)

Kancc (GVR) responded with "Không Sao, Không Chém Gió"

Phase 4: The climax and decline of the battle

V-O released the track "No Skill" - VickyBraak x Rick x S.O.S x Magazine x Đá Đen, dissing GVR

GVR counterattacked strongly with "Ngậm" - Gizmo x SilverC x Da Vickie x BDT x KraziNoyze (S.D Records)

Ryco (V-O) ended with the track "Fuck You Fear"

After this track, the beef gradually calmed down, with no further responses from either side.

RVP + UG9 + S.I.C.K vs. 7LD

Beef between Rap Viet Punchline + Underground Night + S.I.C.K and 7LD

This beef took place at the same time with the 2016 northside vs southside battle, but the focus was on the young rappers of the F4 generation of northside, attracting great attention from the underground community.

Phase 1: Initiation

Initially, 7LD and UG9 only fought friendly, with the following tracks:

"Ngủ Đi Gái" – 291

"Đất Đấy" – M.A.X

The situation became tense when RVP released the MV "Ủa" (MC ILL x Super E), attracting great attention from Underground. 7LD then officially dissed MC ILL with the track "Sầu" – M.A.X x iCD. MC ILL praised M.A.X & iCD, but still responded with "Truyện Cổ Grimm"

Phase 2: UG9 vs. 7LD tensed up

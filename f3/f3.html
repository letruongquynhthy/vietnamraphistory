F3

The third generation is called "F3". F3 rappers are considered who started rapping from 2009 to 2012.

A photo of Wowy (left) and Karik (right)

The downfall of GVR

(2009-2010)

After their golden period, from 2009, GVR began to decline significantly. One of the biggest shocks was when Phương CD stopped rapping after releasing the track “The Good Die Young” (collaboration with VD, Andree, DSK). This seriously affected GVR because Phương CD was one of the most important pillars holding up the organization. Next, Lee7 left GVR after the beef with GODZ to focus on his career in Germany, DSK withdrew from rapping for a while, and Andree left, changed his rap name to Andree Right Hand, and pursued his own path. The loss of these big names caused GVR to fall into a state of serious decline.

Although they tried to maintain the organization with the F2 generation including Rhymastic, Silver C, Ling, etc., this generation of rappers was not strong enough to revive the organization like the previous generation. By the end of 2009, GVR had almost completely disbanded, marking, yet again, the end of a rap empire that once dominated underground Vietnamese rap.

South Ganz

In 2009, South Ganz was founded by Wowy, Young Koo, Baby Buzz, LD, bringing many hits such as “Motorbike”, “Motherfucker Popo”. Later, the group became the launching pad for the careers of Wowy and Karik, two prominent names in the Vietnamese rap scene from 2009-2012.

LadyKillah

After LK's success in the mainstream world (2007 - 2008), he suddenly went into hiding for a long time before returning in March 2010 with the album "Bản Thể 1205" in a completely new rap style. LK, along with JustaTee (formerly known as Jay Tee) and Mr.J—RC's F2 generation rappers, formed LadyKillah through a forum, combining rap and RnB. The group quickly attracted a large number of young people and had many impressive collaborations with Andree Right Hand, Eddy Việt, Cá Chép, Binz through tracks such as "K 2", "Excuse Me", "Call Me The Liar", "Ngọn Nến Trước Gió", "Spaceship".

LadyKillah's representative artists include LK, JustaTee, Emily, Mr.J, Mr.A, BigDaddy, the group F.O.E (Bueno, Mr.T, Yanbi, TMT), Phúc Bồ, Hoàng Tôn, JC Hưng, and Sơn Tùng M-TP, contributing to making LadyKillah a major influential music organization at that time.

G-Fam

The birth of G-Family (G-Fam)

G-Family was founded in 2009 by rapper Acy and members such as BK, Juni, Torich, Gobi, Datmaniac. The group was active during the beef period between major forums. Although, in the present, underground rap is considered to gradually decline, G-Family still maintained its activities because of their skills, solid knowledge of hip-hop and professionalism in their organization. Moreover, the group founded the company G-Century Events to support the rap community and continue to promote the underground movement in Hồ Chí Minh City and the southern region in general.

G-Fam vs. GVR

Beef between G-Family and Genius Viet Rap

During the the declining of dominance, GVR gradually lost its pillars and also their next generation, leaving only the Falling Hill group (after they also disbanded), Dr.BL, Ling and some admins to maintain the forum. Meanwhile, G-Family grew stronger by time. Acy started the beef with the tracks "Letters G", "Đá Lật Đỉnh", "Duyệt Binh" aimed at GVR. GVR responded weakly with Lil' Dyte's track and "GVR Dun Fuckin' Care 3" (Ling), but was looked down upon by the community. G-Family continued to attack with "Ai Đã Khiến Ling Như Vậy", creating a clearly unequal beef.

The formation and disbandment of underground rap organizations since 2009

Việt Underground (VUG):

VUG was founded in 2009 as a forum by 2Ti, Uworm, TiQ, initially just a music community. The organized the VUG Battle King competition to search for talented rappers, notably Worm JB, Pjpo, Khánh Diesel, Ryryu, Kady, Yoki, Lil Zendy. Some notable tracks of the organization include "We Are VietUG's Soul" (Worm JB x Pjpo x Lil' Cov x TiQ), "South West For Life" (Worm JB), "San Bằng Đông Phương" (G-Fam x VUG x TDR – Thủ Đức Rap).

Although it had developed strongly, VUG’s website was hacked, plus the admins and rappers could not continue to operate due to being busy with their personal lives, causing the organization to completely disband.

7Fingers Family:

After Alohiphop disbanded, a number of the new rap organizations emerged in central Vietnam, including 7Fingers Family, founded by Lil’ Kendy (also a member of GVR) through its own forum. The organization's prominent artists include Binz, Sol7, B2C, Lil’ Kendy, LT, N.O. However, after a period of operation, 7Fingers Family could not maintain and officially disbanded.

MSRNation:

After 7Fingers Family disbanded, a new organization emerged in the central region - Midside Rap Nation (MSRNation), attracting many midside rappers such as Honaaz, Mr. Coast, Jay V, Mario, TA, Tornado, Phương NP, C2. The group quickly became the center of the hip-hop community in this region, with outstanding rap songs such as:

"Miền Trung Bọn Tao" – Binz x Jay V

"Dẹp Mặt Trời" – Subby x Binz x Goro x Crisis (dissing DSK in the beef GVR vs. SSR)

"Vẫn Mưa", "Midside Does It" – Jay V x Neway x Coast x Mario

"Life for Rent" – Coast x Phương NP x Tornado x C2

MSRNation did not only have internal beefs but also confronted rappers in the southside region. VD, Sol’Bass, and Goro once released the track “Clean Midside”, which was countered by Jay V with "Ê” track. However, after Binz joined GVR and Jay V went to study abroad, the organization gradually weakened and disbanded eventually when Jay V switched to being a producer, finding success with “Paper Heart Remix – Young H x Quynh Anh”.

After MSRNation disbanded, the midside hip-hop community splitted into many small groups to each region:

Rap34 (Nghệ An province): Lợi Emo, MagicTK, Asa...

Midside43 (Đà Nẵng city): Nico, Coast, N.O, Candy, KidLee, Vkayninezy, WinD... (leaning towards GODZ, often beefing with SSR, GVR, LadyKillah)

Midside75 (Huế city): Mario, LT, Neway, KenC, Nia, Chicken and Fox group, Zesty, Bad B, Lil' Pig (famous with the track "Tau Thích Mi")

PTBoiz (Phan Thiết city): Founded by TA (TAK), including Hata, Thangzet, Bánh Mỳ, Linhkent... Beefing with Ban Me Rap, AoGz, Shood, Phu Yen Boiz, Vietboiz

Bignoize Family (Nha Trang city): SB a.k.a Dizzy, Macthediablo, Wonway, Kay V...

Ban Mê Rap (Buôn Mê Thuột province): Tèo K, ShadowViet, Big Z, Rocky, s0ck, Ken, KyK…

The disbandment of MSRNation marked the transformation of midside rap, from a large organization to many small groups operating independently.

VUG vs. SSR

Beef between Việt Underground and Southside Rap

The conflict between Viet Underground (VUG) and Southside Rap (SSR) started when VUG collaborated with GODZ, resulting in SSR seeing VUG as a rival. When SSR organized the Ready For War tournament, the VUG rappers participating mainly dissed the judges, who were in SSR. Then, the younger groups of SSR (Da Warrious and Đông Phương) dissed VUG, causing VUG, G-Fam and TDR to respond with the track "San Bằng Đông Phương".

The beef officially went to its climax when rapper Kiban left VUG to join SSR, leading to SSR releasing the big track "Lật Thành". VUG responded with "Southside Flow" (Worm JB), "Long Hổ Tung Hoành 2" and "Lên Ngôi 3M" (Pjpo x VD). The Night (SSR) released the track "Xong Rồi", which was countered by Pjpo with "Phó Tổng Thống Cao Kỳ". The war continued to escalate with many diss tracks from both sides such as "Thằng Ngu", "Thời Cơ 2" (VUG), "Tao Địch Mẹ Mày" (DnH x Crimeezy x TiQ x Pjpo), "Uhm! Thì Là Tướng" (SSR).

S.D Records

The birth of Street Doktorz Records (SDR)

After a period of absence, DSK returned to the underground scene and formed the group 21Blackjack with KraziNoyze, Suboi (was called “Quiet Bunny” then), Rapsoul, Nova Lane, DJ Jin, BlakRay. The group later changed their name to Street Doktorz Records (S.D Records) and gained attention with the MV "We Are One". Some of the group's notable tracks include "DMM" (DSK x Lee7 x KraziNoyze), "Haters" (KraziNoyze x Suboi), "Mấy Thằng Khứa" (Quiet Bunny x DSK x KraziNoyze). Later, Rapsoul left the group, and in the track "Kháng Án", DSK mentioned Suboi and Rapsoul but Rapsoul's name was hidden.

D7R

The birth of District7Rap (D7R)

In 2009, D7R was founded by NKq7, Nia, nh0kRain, three rappers from district 7, Hồ Chí Minh City. D7R operated as part of GODZ, an ally of VUG and TDR, mainly beefing with SSR, LadyKillahz and GVR. Some of the group's notable tracks include The Kid" (D7R), "Mơ Tiếp Đi" (VietDragon x Nia x NKq7 x nh0kRain), "Không Thể Dừng Bước" (Leonis x Nia), "Mưa Lạnh" (Nia x NKq7), "Bông Hoa Thêm Phai" (Nia x NKq7), "Hùa SSR" (nh0kRain x NKq7).

TDR

The birth of Thủ Đức Rap (TDR)

Founded in 2010, Thủ Đức Rap (TDR) is a small branch of VUG, focusing on rappers living in district Thủ Đức, Hồ Chí Minh City. Notable members include Kaisoul, Dinny, RazV (Rz"), UnderBlue, T-Time, Master T, Lil TN, Lil Zendy, Nia. Some notable tracks include "Mưa Mùa Hạ" (Kaisoul x Lil TN x RazV), "Kệ Mẹ" (Rz" x Lil TN), "Chán" (Lil TN), "Gửi Em" (Lil TN), "It's Fade" (Rz").

AOGZ

The birth of Angelz of GODZ (AoGz)

In mid-2010, after internal conflicts within GODZ, some members such as BK, Gobi, Datmaniac left to join G-Family, while VD suddenly went silent and unseen online. Rappers loyal to VD formed Angelz of GODZ (AoGz) with the aim of maintaining GODZ’s influence over other organizations such as SSR, GVR, RC.

AoGz includeed rappers such as Lil God, Young Shan, KayV, Yung K, Nia, N-BomB, TiQ, NKq7, and many young rappers from all three regions. They continuously beefed with SSR, SSS, PTboiz, HighlandBoiz, GVR, LadyKillahz, RC, M.A Band, Đông Phương Band, GnD. At the end of 2011, when VD returned, AoGz was merged into GODZ, officially disbanding as AoGz.

Other organizations formed in 2010

Rap1vn (LXRC)

Originally an underground forum in the westside area (meaning the west of southern Vietnam), later renamed Long Xuyên Rap Club (LXRC), representing Long Xuyên province. Main members include Kaisoul, Dinny, s0ck, Rocky, Big Z, Kindly, ShadowViet, Ken, Nhóc Bj, with notable tracks such as "Loving in the Illusionz", "Far Away", "Thu Tàn".

C2R (Củ Chi Rap)

The cradle of many F3 generation rappers, including One T, Young P, Nino N, Trần Trọng Hiếu, Ry2C, B.O.Y. Notable tracks include "Củ Chi Hood", "Đánh Mất Lí Trí", "Vu Vơ Về Đời".

VTR (Vũng Tàu Rap)

They represent the Bà Rịa - Vũng Tàu region, operating through the forum with rappers Jay D, Leebi, NApm, Nhóc Goro, Young Lee, Tyna, Zonki. Known tracks include "Bà Mày Chết Đuối", "Once Again", "Đất Biển Lên Tiếng".

Southside Soldiers (3S)

The group was formed in the southside, gathering rappers such as Vincent Terry, The Night, Black Bi, Kiban, notable with the tracks "Anh Là Ngôi Sao", "Cần".

AnhEmRap (AER)

AER is a rap community organizing the annual show "Tử Tế Show", gathering many artists from Natural Groove, S.D Records, Viet Wizh, SOS Records (Emcee L, Mel G), Tonny Việt. Notable members include Đen, Da LAB, Da Rah! BBC (beatbox), Chicken And Fox.

G-Fam + 3S vs. SSR

Beef between G-Family + Southside Soldiers and Southside Rap

Conflict between both sides originated from the old beef between GODZ and SSR because many G-Fam rappers used to belong to GODZ. The event became intense when SSR organized the show “10 Millionz Starz”, leading to misunderstandings between Halen (SSR) and Acy (G-Fam). SSR’s internal structure then split when Halen wanted to change the admin and forum to Halen.vn, causing the main rappers to protest and split up to form a new organization.

The beef exploded when G-Fam released a series of diss tracks such as "Lật Mặt" (Acy), "Được Bao Lâu" (D N), "Trigger Bigger" (Black Murder x Cá Nóc), "Last Round" (Táo), "Khơi Màu" (various G-Fam artists). 3S also contributed with "I'm Back" (Vincent Terry), "Ừm! Tao Ở Dơ" (Black Bi). SSR responded weakly and gradually became silent, Halen closed the website, stopped rapping, ending the beef.

Small beefs in 2010-2011

Karik vs. VD

The duo Karik and Wowy rose to fame with "2 Thế Giới", "Khu Tao Sống", but when the MV "Khu Tao Sống" was released, VD responded with "Happy New Year" and "Xác Chết". Karik responded with "Underground's Top" (ft. BlakRay) and a series of tracks such as "Thích Thì Diss", "Độc Chiến". The Night, VD's junior group, continued to diss Karik with "Who Is Superstar".

Nah vs. G-Family, The Night

Nah released the VietRap mixtape No1tuloveR, criticized for using Trịnh Công Sơn's music samples (Trịnh Công Sơn was a nationwide songwriter and musician). Many said that Vietnamese rap songs now should invest in making better beats and higher music quality. D N (G-Family) dissed Nah with "But Tao Hated You". The Night, VD, and Acy also targeted Nah.

Andree Right Hand vs. the rap community

After leaving GVR, Andree formed V Taylor Gang, released the album "Sleep Less Work More" but was criticized for remixing American rap music to earn profit as a Vietnamese rapper. For instance, VD and L2K dissed Andree with track "Công Tử Họ Bùi".

Lil' Shady & Kyo vs. Linh Tuti

Linh Tuti caused beef with this duo through "Binladen", "Entry Cho Kyo", but Lil' Shady and Kyo did not respond.

Kiban vs. The Night & Ricky Star

Initially, there was a collab "Ê! Biểu Nè", but when the MV "Con Cá" dissed Kiban, he counterattacked with "Thằng Vô Ơn", "Chó Ăn Kứt" (ft. Charles).

Mr.T vs. BigDaddy

The conflict broke out when Lợi Emo (G-Fam) dissed LadyKillah via "Lấy Máu Hater", BigDaddy responded with "Fuck You T-Bag", then Mr.T counterattacked both of them with "Golden Big Shit", ending the beef.

Tony TK vs. Binz

Originating from a love affair with a girl named Lan, Tony TK diss Binz via "Tàn Canh", but Binz did not respond. Later, the two reconciled and collaborated again with "L", "Sẽ Ra Sao".

16Northside

16NorthSide (originally NorthMusic) was founded in 2011 by Brt, 3Q, TD, mainly to respond a beef with the BMT group (leader Kancc). This beef spreaded, causing the group to confront many other organizations and groups in Hải Phòng city.

In 2012, the group recruited new members and changed its name to 16NorthSide. 3Q participated in R.E.C Cypher season 1 (2013), but season 2 was suspended, causing Brt to not be able to participate. After that, the group continued to operate through shows and cyphers in Hải Phòng.

Other organizations formed in 2012

Highland’s Boiz (HLBz)

Represent rap in the Vietnam central highlands, including Goro, Triple K, Real Man, Mr.Max, Minhphucpk, T2K, S.O.S, Nhon, Loren Kid. Notable tracks: "Vương Tượng", "R.I.P Triple K", "I And Rap" (Minhphucpk).

Free Spirit Rap (FSR) – The disbandment of SSR

After Halen changed SSR to his liking, the main rappers protested and left to form FSR (2012) including Torai9, 2P (Pjpo), Kiban, Young H, Macthediablo, Black Bi, Zinnine, Charles, Riki, Kady, Kiddy, L.J, Spyder, Phutoro, Baby Red. 49 Soldiers

Founded in Lâm Đồng province, including Will, Ryco, Rick, Khói, YunStar. Used to beef with HLBz, G5R, 3S. After a period of operation, the members separated, causing 49 Soldiers to disband.

Freedomer Team (FDT)

Founded on March 15, 2012 in Đồng Nai city, operating with the motto of freedom, not following a framework. Members include Magazine (leader), Kendj, Jackbi, Wako, Sben, Leo, 230d, Zinbi, Killbee, Willbee, Pysun, Zury, Kjby. Gangfive Rap (G5R)

They rose to fame after beefing with FSR, FDT. Notable tracks: "Westside Squad" (Jombie x Endless x Dế Choắt), "Family is All" (Jombie x Endless x Tkan), "Hai Lúa Tây Du" (Jombie x LeeYang x Sâu).

3BGangz

Gathered westside rappers such as 2P, Kenbj, Dyte, Minh Anh, Tbi, SD, Zinnine. Notable tracks: "ĐM Thằng Bạn" (2P x Zinnine), "Thiên Bình" (Kenbj), "Nơi Em Thuộc Về" (2P x Ricky Star), "Em Là Mưa".

CMK

Rap group in An Giang province, including Mr.QD, WillyCP, Uy Men, Tken, T.v.R, MPL. Notable tracks: "Rap Việt 2013" (WillyCP), "Bao Lâu Rồi" (Mr.QD x Uy Men), "Thật" (Uy Men).

Luxury Bass

Born on September 2, 2012, aiming at mainstream hip-hop, including LangzLD (LangzBuzze), MTK, R.Tee, Nax, T.I.C. Notable tracks: "A!D$" (LangzBuzze x Nax x Brytinz), "Over" (Black Murder x Roy P x MTK), "Say La La" (T.I.C), "Ông Vua Trong Hood Nhỏ" (Young H x LangzBuzze x Worm JB).

G-Fam vs. HLBz

Beef between G-Family and Highland's Boiz

The beef started when Highland's Boiz (HLBz) released the big track "Vương Tượng", Acy (G-Family) responded with "Săn Thú", then HLBz released "2H12" and Acy counterattacked with "1968". Acy's homies also joined in with "Shut Up" (Sol'Bass x Acy), "Street Energy" (Lợi Emo x Black Murder), "Thôi Xong 2". The beef spreaded when G5R, 3S joined in, notably with "Chấp Hết" (Jombie x Young P x Endless x ECS), "Nhọn Ơi" (The Night), and the beed finally ended in early 2013.

G5R vs. FSR

Beef between Gangfive Rap and Free Spirit Rap

Both G5R and FSR were formed at the same time, causing conflict. G5R opened the beef with "Young Hôi Lông" (Jombie dissed FSR & Young H), Young H countered with "Mấy Thằng Phế Vật". Torai9 x 2P responded with "Khoảng Cách", while G5R continued with "Cuối Xuống Đi 2 Già" (Jombie x Afan). FSR retaliated with "Bá Vương Đồ" (Torai9 x VickyBraak) and "Trăn Xào Lăn" (Kady x Torai9 x Charles). The two sides continued to insult each other through tracks like "Westside G" (Kiban x 2P), before the beef gradually cooled down.

The return of Genius Viet Rap

In 2012, GVR returned thanks to the efforts of the forum admins, Linh Lam and Lee7, inviting back many of the main rappers of F1, F2. However, Andree Right Hand and DSK opposed their new way of operation, leading to internal conflicts. GVR's return was marked by the tracks:

“Tam Kỳ’s Pride" (Binz x Linh Lam x Lee7), "La La La" (Linh Lam x Binz), "My Name Is GVR" (Rhymastic)

"Genius Viet Rap 2012" with Lil' Shady, Lee7, Linh Lam, Kay-T, BDT, Lil' Cì, It's Lee, Binz, DK Love, Beezy (Dr.BL), Dr.Crizzle

GVR also successfully organized DLD GVR 2012, finding the next GVR generation such as Kancc, Gizmo, Triple K, LT, One T, B-Ray, B-Wine, Dizzy, along with notable rappers of this period such as Lynk Lee, Silver C, 3T (PXT), Da Vickie, T-Akayz.

The death of VD

& the disbandment of GODZ

After GVR returned, VD also returned to the organization, reconciled with Lee7 and collaborated with Linh Lam, DK Love through the tracks "My Way", "Your Intention", "Dừng Lại", "Việt Tích Bằng Y Remix". He also released the track "A Call From Another World", apologizing to the people he used to diss, such as DSK, Tony TK, Thai Viet G, Khanh Nhỏ.

When the MV "The Legend" was about to be filmed, news of VD's death appeared, but many people thought it was a troll, a lie. Only when Linh Lam confirmed it from VD's family did the underground community believe it was true. Many rappers like Acy, Halen, Nah decided to stop their hatred for him. Tribute tracks like "Tưởng Tượng" (Halen), "3 Năm" were released, but Acy created a small beef with the track "Everybody Like A King" as a response to Halen, then he released "1008", which featured VD's voice in the demo sent before.

DSK freestyled in memory of VD and deleted all of his diss tracks. The cause of VD's death is still a mystery, there is news that he committed suicide with his friend, but his friend was saved. After this event, GODZ gradually disbanded, but Young VD continued the second generation with Masta D, Dr.Yan, The Light. He also beefed with B Ray, but lost the battle with the track "ĐV VD" (B Ray).

2011-2012

Mainstream rap scene

In the period of 2011 - 2012, mainstream Vietnam witnessed the appearance of many new rappers such as Hoàng Rapper, Tronie (who used to be in Freestyle Crew with Karik). Karik won the Best Video award at MTV Vietnam with the MV "Xã Hội Rắc Rối". LadyKillah expanded their cooperation with the V-Pop genre, standing out with the hit "Thu Cuối" (Mr.T x Yanbi x Hang Bingboong).

Suboi left Music Faces, released the album "Run". Đen and JGKiD won the 2011 Vietnamese Song Award with "Cây Bàng". Zing Music Awards 2011 awarded "Tau Thích Mi" (Lil' Pig), "Nothing In Your Eyes" (Mr.T x Yanbi), while "Thu Cuối" dominated ZMA 2012 with 3 awards. Other songs such as "Ngọn Nến Trước Gió" (LK x JustaTee x Andree), "Beautiful Girl" (Cường 7 x Mr.A), "Cơn Mưa Ngang Qua" (M-TP) were also honored.

After serving time in prison due to gang conflicts, Thai Viet G founded 454 Life Entertainment in the US, collaborating with Vietnamese rappers through "Hold You Down" (Thai Viet G x Suboi), "Khép Lại" (Thai Viet G x J.Lee - GVR).

Midside Pride

In 2012, Lil'Tc, Harryz, Vic founded Midside Pride (mspride.vn) to represent the midside hip-hop community. Notable members include Lil'Tc, Jay V, Binz, Cá Chép, Lil' G, with typical tracks such as "We're Midside Boiz" (Jay V x Binz x Lil' G), "M.O (Midside Original)" (Jay V x Binz, in beef with HLBz). However, when organizing the battle, the website was hacked, making Midside Pride the last forum-based rap organization of the midside region.

South Pride Rap

Founded in 2012, gathering many young rappers such as Nou9z, Gino, Lil' Ken, Dark, SG Prider (including JackT, P.Shi, Yuno Bigboi, DJ Bin Younger), DC GanG (including Sol7, V-Soul, Yuno SK, NowB, TB, MadBee, Locie$, P$mall). They are known through the tracks "Imma South Pride", "Return", mixtape "Ngục".

93 Hood

DVG

The birth of DaVagrants-Art (DVG)
